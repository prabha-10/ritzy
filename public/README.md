# How to Add Your Logo

## Step-by-Step Instructions:

1. **Locate your logo PNG file** on your computer

2. **Drag and drop** your logo PNG file into this `public` folder

3. **Rename** the file to `logo.png` (or update the Header.tsx to use your filename)

4. **Refresh** your browser at http://localhost:3000/

Your logo will automatically appear in the header!

## Recommended Logo Specifications:

- **Format:** PNG with transparent background
- **Size:** 200×200 pixels or larger (will be scaled down to 40×40px)
- **File name:** `logo.png`
